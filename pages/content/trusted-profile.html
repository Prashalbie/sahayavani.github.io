Trusted Profile
Profil Zaufany

Last updated: June 2025 · Verified against pz.gov.pl

💡 Profil Zaufany (PROH-feel zah-oo-FAH-nih) = "Trusted Profile." Poland's official digital identity — like India's Aadhaar for digital signatures. Free, takes 15 minutes, unlocks almost all government services online.

Before you start — what you need

  • A numer PESEL (NOO-mer PEH-sel) — mandatory
  • Polish bank account OR Polish phone number
  • Passport
  • Email address
⚠️ No PESEL = no Profil Zaufany. Visit your Urząd Gminy (OO-zhond GMEE-nih = municipal office) with your passport and rental agreement to get PESEL first.

Method 1 — Via your Polish bank (easiest)

If you bank with PKO BP, mBank, Santander, ING, Pekao, or Alior — this takes under 5 minutes with no office visit.

1

Go to pz.gov.pl

Visit pz.gov.pl → and click "Załóż Profil Zaufany" (Zah-WOOJ PROH-feel zah-oo-FAH-nih).

2

Choose "przez bank" (pshes bank)

Select your bank from the list. You'll be redirected to your bank's login page.

3

Log in and confirm

Your bank verifies identity automatically using your PESEL. Confirm the Profil Zaufany creation. Done — under 5 minutes.

Method 2 — In person at a government office

If you don't have a Polish bank account yet, visit one of these offices with your passport and PESEL:

  • Urząd Gminy / Urząd Miasta (OO-zhond GMEE-nih / OO-zhond MYAH-stah) — City / Municipal Hall
  • ZUS (ZOO-s) — Social insurance office
  • Urząd Skarbowy (OO-zhond skar-BOH-vih) — Tax office
  • Oddział NFZ (OD-jow en-ef-ZET) — NFZ branch
  • Poczta Polska (POCH-tah POL-skah) — Main post offices
1

Register online first at pz.gov.pl

You'll receive a numer referencyjny (NOO-mer reh-fer-en-TSIY-nih = reference number).

2

Visit the office within 14 days

Bring passport and reference number. The official confirms your identity and activates your profile. 5 minutes at the counter.

What can you do with Profil Zaufany?

  • Apply for and track Karta Pobytu online via gov.pl →
  • Submit annual tax return — zeznanie podatkowe / PIT (peet)
  • Register children in Polish schools — szkoła (SHKOH-wah)
  • Access Internet Patient Account — IKP (ee-kah-PEH) for medical records
  • Check your ZUS contributions — składki ZUS (SKWAD-kee ZOO-s)
  • Report change of address — zmiana meldunku (ZMYAH-nah mel-DOON-koo)
  • Sign official documents digitally — podpisz dokumenty (pod-PEESH doh-koo-MEN-tih)

mObywatel app — carry your ID on your phone

mObywatel (em-oh-BY-vah-tel) — download after creating Profil Zaufany. Stores your digital ID and Karta Pobytu data. Increasingly accepted at hotels and some offices.
